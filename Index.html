EPA Air Quality Index (AQI) Levels

The EPA AQI is divided into six categories indicating air quality levels:

  • Good (0-50): Air quality is satisfactory
  • Moderate (51-100): Acceptable quality
  • Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups (101-150)
  • Unhealthy (151-200)
  • Very Unhealthy (201-300)
  • Hazardous (301-500)
